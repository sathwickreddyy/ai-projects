# Decision: 4-Step Review Modal (Not Inline Panel)

**Date:** 2026-03-25
**Status:** Accepted

Evaluated two review UX models:
- A) Panel-based (always visible on right side) — quick but cramped
- B) Full-screen modal with guided steps — focused, more space for diff view

Chose B: full-screen modal with 4 steps:
1. Score & Summary (streaming + parsed result)
2. Side-by-Side Diff (v1 vs current, added/removed highlighted)
3. Suggestions & Follow-up (missing components + questionnaire)
4. Skill Adaptation (approve learnings back to skill files)

Right panel still exists for quick access to suggestions/follow-up/adapt during normal canvas work.
